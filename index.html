Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the monthly rent?

All utilities, high-speed WiFi, weekly cleaning of common areas, and access to all building amenities are included in your monthly rent.

What are the lease terms?

We offer flexible lease terms starting from 3 months, with discounts for longer commitments. Most residents choose 6-12 month leases.

Is there a security deposit?

Yes, we require a security deposit equal to one month's rent, which is fully refundable upon move-out, subject to our terms and conditions.
